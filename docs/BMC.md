# Business Model Canvas
## Value Proposition
Offline-First App provides a seamless user experience by allowing users to create boards and add ideas to them even when offline. The app syncs with a server when connectivity is restored, ensuring that data is always up-to-date. This feature is particularly useful for users who need to access their data in areas with limited or no internet connectivity.

## Customer Segments
* Remote workers and freelancers who need to access their data on-the-go
* Students and researchers who require access to their data in areas with limited internet connectivity
* Small business owners who need to manage their projects and ideas offline
* Individuals who value data security and want to ensure that their data is not compromised by online connectivity issues

## Channels
* Web application (online and offline)
* Mobile application (online and offline)
* API for integration with other applications

## Revenue Streams
* Subscription-based model for individuals and small businesses
* Enterprise licensing for large businesses and organizations
* Advertising revenue from sponsored boards and ideas
* Data analytics and insights for businesses and organizations

## Cost Structure
* Development and maintenance costs for the offline-first app
* Server and infrastructure costs for syncing data
* Marketing and advertising expenses
* Salaries and benefits for development and support teams

## Key Resources
* Development team with expertise in offline-first app development
* Server and infrastructure for syncing data
* Marketing and sales teams for promoting the app
* Partnerships with other businesses and organizations for integration and collaboration

## Key Activities
* Developing and maintaining the offline-first app
* Marketing and promoting the app to target customer segments
* Building and maintaining partnerships with other businesses and organizations
* Providing customer support and maintenance for the app

## Key Partners
* Server and infrastructure providers for syncing data
* Marketing and advertising agencies for promoting the app
* Partnerships with other businesses and organizations for integration and collaboration
* Development and support teams for maintaining the app

## Key Partnerships
* Partnership with a server and infrastructure provider for syncing data
* Partnership with a marketing and advertising agency for promoting the app
* Partnership with a development and support team for maintaining the app
* Partnership with other businesses and organizations for integration and collaboration

## Revenue Streams Breakdown
* Subscription-based model: 60%
* Enterprise licensing: 20%
* Advertising revenue: 10%
* Data analytics and insights: 10%

## Cost Structure Breakdown
* Development and maintenance costs: 40%
* Server and infrastructure costs: 20%
* Marketing and advertising expenses: 20%
* Salaries and benefits for development and support teams: 20%
