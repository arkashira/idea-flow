## Product: **idea‑flow** – Collaborative Idea Management & Prioritisation  

| Phase | Trigger Event | Friction Points | User Emotions | Opportunities to Delight | Success Metric |
|------|---------------|----------------|---------------|--------------------------|----------------|
| **Aware** | • A team member sees a LinkedIn post about “‑10× faster brainstorming with AI‑driven idea boards”.<br>• An internal email from HR announcing a new “Innovation Sprint” program. | • Low brand recall (idea‑flow is unknown).<br>• Over‑crowded content feeds (many productivity tools). | Curiosity → Skepticism | • Eye‑catching micro‑video (30 s) showing a live brainstorming session turning 30 raw ideas into a ranked backlog in 5 min.<br>• Free “Idea‑Sprint Playbook” PDF that maps the sprint to the user’s calendar. | **Impression‑to‑Click Rate** ≥ 4 % (target 5 % after 3 months). |
| **Consider** | • User clicks the ad/PDF link and lands on the product landing page. | • Unclear differentiation from generic note‑apps (e.g., Notion, Miro).<br>• No pricing transparency. | Interest → Overwhelm | • Interactive demo that lets the visitor drop 3 dummy ideas and watch the AI auto‑cluster and vote‑rank them.<br>• Transparent “Starter” pricing table (Free‑tier, Team‑tier $12/user/mo). | **Landing‑Page Conversion** (download demo / sign‑up) ≥ 12 % (baseline 8 %). |

| **Expand** | • Paid team uses idea‑flow for multiple projects and requests deeper integrations. | • Integration fatigue (multiple tools, custom APIs).<br>• Lack of advanced analytics (e.g., ROI per idea). | Confidence → Advocacy | • Pre‑built integrations: Jira, Asana, Trello, Microsoft Teams, Google Workspace.<br>• Advanced analytics dashboard (idea churn, time‑to‑decision, revenue impact).<br>• Referral program: “Invite a teammate, get 1 month free”. | **Expansion Rate** (upsell to Enterprise tier or add‑on features) ≥ 15 % of existing paid accounts per quarter. |

### Narrative Flow (High‑Level)

1. **Aware** – The prospect discovers idea‑flow through targeted content that highlights the speed and AI‑assisted structuring of brainstorming sessions.  
2. **Consider** – They land on a purpose‑built page that instantly demonstrates the core value proposition and removes price ambiguity.  
3. **Try** – A friction‑free free tier and guided onboarding let the user experience the core loop (capture → cluster → vote → prioritize) within a week.  
4. **Adopt** – Positive sprint outcomes trigger a natural upgrade to unlock collaboration and export capabilities, reinforced by data‑driven insights.  
5. **Expand** – Deep integrations and analytics turn idea‑flow into a strategic hub, encouraging cross‑team adoption and referrals.
