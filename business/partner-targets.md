# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for Idea-Flow

### Target Integrations

| Partner/SaaS/API        | Free-Tier Limits               | Integration Effort | Value-Add (User Job Solved)                               | Rationale for Integration                                     |
|-------------------------|-------------------------------|--------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Trello**              | Free for 10 boards           | M                  | Organizing and prioritizing ideas into actionable tasks   | Trello is widely used for task management; integration enhances idea tracking and execution. |
| **Slack**               | Free for 10k messages        | S                  | Real-time collaboration and feedback on ideas             | Slack is a popular communication tool; integration allows seamless sharing and discussion of ideas. |
| **Asana**               | Free for 15 users            | M                  | Task assignment and progress tracking                      | Asana helps teams manage projects; integration allows for direct transformation of ideas into tasks. |
| **Zapier**              | Free for 5 Zaps              | L                  | Automating workflows between Idea-Flow and other apps     | Zapier connects various apps; integration enables users to automate idea management processes. |
| **Google Drive**        | 15 GB storage                 | S                  | Storing and sharing documents related to ideas            | Google Drive is widely used for document management; integration facilitates easy access to related files. |
| **Notion**              | Free for personal use         | M                  | Centralized documentation and organization of ideas       | Notion combines note-taking and task management; integration enhances idea documentation and collaboration. |
| **Monday.com**          | Free for 2 users             | M                  | Visual project tracking and management                     | Monday.com provides a visual interface for project management; integration aids in tracking idea implementation. |
| **Airtable**            | Free for 1,200 records        | M                  | Database management for ideas and feedback collection      | Airtable offers a flexible database solution; integration allows for structured idea management and analysis. |

### Prioritization Rationale
1. **Trello** and **Slack** are prioritized due to their widespread use and ability to enhance collaboration and task management directly related to idea execution.
2. **Asana** and **Zapier** follow, as they provide essential project management capabilities and automation, respectively, which can significantly streamline workflows.
3. **Google Drive** and **Notion** are included for their document management and organization capabilities, supporting the documentation of ideas and feedback.
4. **Monday.com** and **Airtable** are valuable for their visual project tracking and database management features, which can further enhance the idea management process.

### Conclusion
Integrating with these platforms will not only enhance the functionality of Idea-Flow but also provide users with a seamless experience in managing their brainstorming ideas and turning them into actionable plans. Prioritizing partnerships with affiliate/revenue-share opportunities will also help in monetizing the integrations effectively.
