
# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
*The collaborative idea management tool that turns brainstorming chaos into actionable plans for teams.*
*Alternatives:*
  1. For startups: The agile idea pipeline that helps turn brainstorming into product roadmaps.
  2. For enterprises: The structured innovation platform that manages and prioritizes R&D ideas.
  3. For creative agencies: The collaborative brainstorming tool that organizes client ideas into projects.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Personas)
- **Alex, Product Manager at a mid-sized tech startup (3 years experience)**  
  Daily job: Leads product strategy, manages feature backlog, collaborates with engineering.  
  Budget: $5,000 - $10,000 annually for collaboration and project management tools.  
- **Maria, Innovation Lead at a large corporation (10 years experience)**  
  Daily job: Oversees R&D initiatives, manages cross-functional teams, tracks innovation metrics.  
  Budget: $20,000 - $50,000 annually for enterprise collaboration and innovation tools.  
- **Sam, Team Lead at a creative agency (5 years experience)**  
  Daily job: Leads brainstorming sessions, manages client projects, ensures creative alignment.  
  Budget: $8,000 - $15,000 annually for project management and collaboration tools.

## Channels (Top 3 with CAC Estimate)
1. **LinkedIn Ads**: Target "Product Manager", "Innovation Lead", "Team Lead" with job titles and company size (1k-5k, 5k-10k). CAC estimate: $300.  
2. **Content Marketing**: Publish case studies, webinars, and blog posts on Product Hunt, TechCrunch, and industry-specific blogs (e.g., Product Management, Innovation). CAC estimate: $150.  
3. **Referral Program**: Leverage existing Axentx customers (e.g., AI red-teaming service users) to refer new customers. Offer a $100 referral bonus. CAC estimate: $100.

## Content Cadence (Week-by-Week)
- **Week 1**: Publish blog post "The 3 Biggest Mistakes in Idea Management and How to Avoid Them". Share on LinkedIn, Twitter.  
- **Week 2**: Launch beta program with 50 early adopters (selected via LinkedIn). Send welcome email with onboarding guide.  
- **Week 3**: Publish case study of a startup using idea-flow to launch a new product. Share on Product Hunt.  
- **Week 4**: Host webinar "From Brainstorming to Action: A Step-by-Step Guide to Prioritizing Ideas". Promote via email list and LinkedIn.  
- **Week 5**: Create infographic "The Idea Management Workflow: From Capture to Execution". Share on Twitter and LinkedIn.  
- **Week 6**: Publish guest post on a marketing blog (e.g., HubSpot) about collaborative innovation.  
- **Week 7**: Release video tutorial on the collaboration features. Share on YouTube and LinkedIn.  
- **Week 8**: Publish user testimonial video from an early adopter. Share on social media.  
- **Week 9**: Launch email newsletter with weekly tips on idea management.  
- **Week 10**: Run social media campaign with user-generated content (UGC) prompts.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30**: Beta launch to 50 early adopters. Conduct feedback sessions and iterate on features.  
- **D-0**: Official product launch with pricing ($99/month for teams up to 10 users, $199/month for teams 11-50 users). Launch on website and via email to the beta list.  
- **D+30**: Reach 200 paying users. Launch referral program. Start tracking DAU.  
- **D+90**: Achieve $50k MRR. Expand to new markets (e.g., Thailand) based on TAM analysis.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU**: Target 500 daily active users by D+90.  
- **$MRR Target**: $50,000 MRR by D+90.
